Courses We Offer

Our University offers a diverse range of courses from intermediate to postgraduate levels, designed to cater to various academic interests and career goals. Each program is crafted with a balance of theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring students are well-prepared for future opportunities.

Intermediate

Our intermediate (Inter) courses provide a strong foundation for higher education, offering a range of subjects across various streams. In the Science stream, students can study Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, laying the groundwork for careers in engineering, medicine, and research. The Commerce stream includes subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics, preparing students for business, finance, and management fields. The Arts stream offers courses in History, Political Science, Sociology, and Psychology, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills essential for careers in social sciences and humanities. These comprehensive courses are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for advanced studies and diverse career paths.

Degree

Degree Courses Offered
Our University offers diverse degree programs including Bachelor of Arts (English, History, Psychology), Bachelor of Science (Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science), Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting, Finance, Marketing), and Bachelor of Business Administration (HR Management, International Business). We also provide specialized degrees in Technology (BTech), Fine Arts (BFA), Social Work (BSW), and Education (BEd), ensuring a well-rounded academic foundation for successful careers.g

Post Graduation

Our University offers a range of postgraduate courses to enhance academic and professional expertise. Students can pursue a Master of Arts or Master of Science with specializations in subjects like English, History, Physics, and Computer Science. The Master of Commerce program focuses on Accounting, Finance, and Marketing, while the Master of Business Administration provides advanced knowledge in areas such as Finance and Human Resource Management. Additionally, we offer Master of Technology degrees in fields like Software Engineering and Data Science, as well as Master of Fine Arts, Social Work, and Education programs to prepare students for advanced roles in their respective fields.
